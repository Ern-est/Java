Akan names profile-Ghana.

Akan names are given to kids according to days of the week.

Akan is community located in Ghana and are famously known for their strange culture of naming their kids after days of the week unlike most of African communities. Akan names further are created through twin status,birth order and even their ancestor's middle names but are specifically middle names.However,"day names" not only represent day a child was born but also have other meanings concerning character and the child's soul.Furthermore,this naming system is also used by other west African countries and also africans abroad.
